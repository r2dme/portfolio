S.A.F.E Augmented Reality App

Bringing workplace safety out of documents and training schedules. Look around you.

Category

Mobile AR App


Technology

Adobe XD, C# Programming, Unity, Vuforia


Date

June 2019

S.A.F.E. App provides access to an easy to use database of machinery safety, work place safety information accessible via AR. This app prototype could be used by a Governmental body or a company to ensure workplace and machinery safety accessed by every employee quickly. Real-time image recognition will provide safety information overlaid on real world machines. The image recognition is built using Vuforia framework.

VirtualFolio Virtual Reality Experience

Experience Artists & Artworks the way they are intended to be. Immersed.

Category

VR Application


Technology

Unity, C# Programming, SteamVR, HTC Vive Pro


Date

October 2019

An immersive & interactive portfolio platform where artists can exhibit their artworks in environments where they would like viewer to experience it. A simple catalogue that contains all artworks links the artwork experiences together. This enables independent artists to exhibit their work wherever they like without worrying about contractors and let the viewer experience it from the comfort of their home. The demo was built to run in HTC Vive Pro.

What the fork Recipe Website

Sharing culture through food, one recipe at a time.

Category

Website / Community project


Technology

HTML, CSS, Social Media, Subscriptions


Date

October 2019

Built to be a culture exchange initiative, What the fork recipe sharing website enables people from around the world to share their culture through traditional recipes. This project is the brain child of three students from different cultures who were united by their love for food. I was responsible for developing the responsive website and managing instagram. Visit the site here - whatfork.github.io.
